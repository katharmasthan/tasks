Types Of Article

Definite Article

"Definite article" redirects here. For the comedy album, see Definite Article. definite article is an article that marks a definite noun phrase. Definite articles, such as the English the, are used to refer to a paricular member of a group. It may be something that the speaker has already mentioned, or it may be something uniquely specified. example, Sentence 1 uses the definite article and thus expresses a request for a particular book. In contrast, Sentence 2 uses an finite article and thus conveys that the speaker would be satisfied with any book. 1. Give me the book. 2. Give me a book. definite article can also be used in English to indicate a specific class among other classes: The cabbage white butterfly lays its eggs on members of the Brassica genus. vever, recent developments show that definite articles are morphological elements linked to certain noun types due to calization. Under this point of view, definiteness does not play a role in the selection of a definite article more than the lexical entry
